The Social Army group's important project, connected with the heart, is called the "Live Blood Bank". In this project, as you all know, during emergencies or when there is a need for specific blood groups, it becomes challenging to find suitable donors. To address this challenge, the Live Blood Bank project connects a vast network of blood donors across various cities and states through a centralized system. These donors selflessly offer blood to serve others. Now, the Live Blood Bank is being relaunched with a fresh look and interface, where you all can witness a new web platform. If you wish to contribute blood altruistically, you can register on the website and receive information. Additionally, a mobile application for the Live Blood Bank is also in the pipeline, which will make it easier for you to arrange blood donations during emergencies. You will also be able to donate blood conveniently through this application.

WHY DONATE BLOOD?

Every two seconds, someone in the world needs blood. By donating blood, you can save lives and help those in critical need. Blood donations are essential for surgeries, cancer treatments, chronic illnesses, and traumatic injuries. Your single donation can save up to three lives!
